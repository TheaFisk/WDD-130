Remote Control Airplane

My RC plane project is a hands-on exploration of aerodynamics, electronics, and craftsmanship. I designed and built a remote-controlled aircraft from the ground up, incorporating lightweight materials, efficient propulsion systems, and precise control surfaces to optimize flight performance. Throughout the project, I experimented with different wing configurations, motor setups, and radio frequencies to enhance stability and maneuverability. This endeavor not only deepened my understanding of flight mechanics but also honed my problem-solving and engineering skills. Whether fine-tuning aerodynamics or troubleshooting electronic components, this project reflects my passion for aviation and innovation in model aircraft design.
