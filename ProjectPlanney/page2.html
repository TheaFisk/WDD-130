Radios

I love learning, and Radio technology gives me a fantastic opportunity to do so! I love the feeling of making contacts around the globe while I devlop my skills as a radio technician.

Pictured here is my HT (Handheld Transceiver). I use this radio to keep in contact with people in the area and check into nets.

This peice of technology is called a Meshtastic Node. It's a transmitter using open source software to function, and you don't need a license to use it.

Radio equipment is kind of expensive! Someday I hope to have an impressive setup, but this is what I have for now!
